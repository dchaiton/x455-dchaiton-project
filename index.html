Hotels & Resorts

Often called the “Castle in the Rockies,” the Fairmont Banff Springs is one of Canada’s most iconic hotels, providing luxury and history in a stunning, castle-like setting. It offers a full-service spa, numerous dining options, and easy access to outdoor activities. Located close to downtown Banff, the Banff Park Lodge offers comfortable lodging with convenient access to the town’s shops, restaurants, and attractions. It features spacious rooms and excellent amenities, including an indoor pool, sauna, and steam room, making it a great family-friendly option.
